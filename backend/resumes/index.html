How Matrix Came to Be

Matrix Enterprises was established in 2020 by Myles Munroe after a deeply personal experience with being hacked. That moment made him realise how fragile digital privacy can be, and it sparked a mission to help people and organisations protect what matters most.

What began as a response to a painful personal setback grew into a purpose-driven company focused on privacy, security, and trust. Myles wanted to build something that would help others stay safe online and feel confident in the systems they use every day.

With support from friends like Wesley Waoudo and a close circle of trusted collaborators, Matrix was created to turn that experience into a mission: securing privacy, strengthening digital resilience, and making security feel accessible.
